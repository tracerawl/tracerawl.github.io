why we exist

We're on a mission to eliminate burnout, optimize productivity, and make you happier.

We spend 1/3rd of our lives working. Stress, anxiety, and burnout are now shrugged off as unavoidable byproducts of our work culture. We help you balance your work with activities that let your mind rest. This not only makes you happier and healthier, but equipped to bring more focus, clarity, and compassion to your work.

how it works

We bring our mobile meditation studio to you and transform your space into a tranquil retreat.

Meditation is a simple, convenient, and delightful activity that helps us center and embody our best selves. Modern scientific research has proven that meditation can physically alter our minds and bodies in incredible ways. We get to know you and provide meditation lessons and guidance that are centered around your goals, needs, and abilities.

Why meditation?

There are many ways to improve our wellbeing and be our best selves. At Sadhana, we believe a consistent meditation practice is the single most powerful tool we can use to improve in all other aspects of our lives. Here's why:

Simple

Contrary to popular belief, learning to meditate isn't difficult with the right guidance.

Convenient

You can meditate anywhere and at any time! On your commute, at your desk, in bed before falling asleep, even while eating.

Delightful

Once you pass over the initial hurdle and make meditation a  habit, you'll be surprised and delighted at the effects is has on your wellbeing.

I'm Trace, and I want to make you happier!
